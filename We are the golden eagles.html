WE ARE THE GOLDEN EAGLES

The Accra New Town adventist Youth Society (AYS&AJY),now
Adventist Youth Ministry was organised on October 1979. On 17th September,2016 its Junior Ministry was branded and named "The Golden Eagles Pathfinder Club".The purpose is to make the
local pathfinder club very attractive to children in and aroud our vicinity/community. With this,The Golden Eagles was
the first pathfinder club in club-naming and branding in Ghana.

We are happy that you have an interest in our Youth Ministry.
As you read on we hope you find more interesting details.
We are the Golden Eagles Pathfinder Club of the Accra New Town Seventh-day Adventist Church.
We are the Accra City Conference,under the Southern Ghana Union Conference
of the West-central Africa Division.
Our goal is to build up the youths in our church and community with a Spiritual foundation of knowing Jesus Christ and understanding of
Godlines personal knowledge of Christ.
