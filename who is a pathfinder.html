WHO IS A PATHFINDER?

If you are 10 to 15 years old and want to make friends,have fun
and challenge yourself,thenPathfinder club is for you!

If you want to discover your true self in camping,hiking and other
outdoor activities,we do heaps of that! Along the way,you'll learn
skills such as tent pitching,knot tying,cooking,fire building,first aid etc.

If you like earning badges and awards, there are six badge levels to
work through, and dozens of honour patches to collect along the way.
Whether your interest is the natural world, arts and crafts, life
skills or physical activities, there are honours in all these areas and more.

You wont get bored being a Golden Eagle Pathfinder Club member.We do ceremonies,marching and drilling.
We do games.We get dirty and tired.We build your confidence level,self independent assertiveness
and your leadership skills. We contribute to our local community.And we also honour our God in heaven who makes it all possible.
